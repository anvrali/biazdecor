Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Biaz Decor collects, uses and protects your information when you visit this website, submit the contact form, or reach out to us via WhatsApp, phone or email.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact form details: name, phone number, and the message/project details you choose to share.
  • WhatsApp conversations: messages, phone number and any details you send us when you tap a WhatsApp button on this site.
  • Site usage data: basic, non-identifying browser and device information collected automatically to keep the site running smoothly.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries about products, construction or renovation projects.
  • To prepare quotations, schedules and order confirmations.
  • To share updates about an ongoing order or project.
  • To improve this website and our services.

3. WhatsApp Communication

When you click a WhatsApp button on this site, you are redirected to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. Sending that message is your choice, and the conversation that follows is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy in addition to this one.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Details may be shared only with team members or contractors directly involved in fulfilling your order or project, and where required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain enquiry and project details only for as long as needed to complete the order or project and to meet any legal or accounting requirements afterward.

6. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use browser storage (cookies or local storage) to track visitors. Any embedded map or third-party content (such as Google Maps) may set its own cookies according to its provider's policy.

7. Your Choices

You may ask us to update or delete the personal details we hold about you at any time by contacting us using the details below.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us, though no method of transmission over the internet or WhatsApp is completely secure.

9. Children's Privacy

This site and our services are intended for adults making enquiries about construction, renovation, or decor products, and are not directed at children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, reach us at hello@biazdecor.com or via the WhatsApp button on this site.
